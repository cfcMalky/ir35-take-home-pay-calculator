What’s the Difference?

Limited Company

  • You run your own business
  • Paid via salary + dividends
  • More tax efficient outside IR35

Umbrella Company

  • You are an employee
  • Paid through PAYE
  • Simple but lower take-home

IR35 Explained

IR35 determines how you're taxed. Inside IR35 means PAYE-style tax. Outside IR35 allows more tax efficiency.

Which Should You Choose?

  • New contractor → Umbrella
  • Experienced & higher rate → Limited
  • Want simplicity → Umbrella
  • Want maximum income → Limited

Real Example

A contractor earning £400/day will typically take home more through a limited company, especially outside IR35.

FAQs

Is a limited company better?

Usually yes outside IR35 due to tax efficiency.

Do umbrella companies pay less?

Yes, due to PAYE taxation and additional costs.
